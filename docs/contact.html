Frequently Asked Questions

What are the dining hall operating hours?
The dining hall operates daily with the following schedule: Breakfast (7:00-9:00 AM), Lunch (1:00-2:30 PM), and Dinner (7:30-9:00 PM). On Sundays, breakfast starts at 8:00 AM.
How do I register for a meal plan?
You can register for a meal plan through the student dashboard after logging into the system. Navigate to "Meal Plan" section and select your preferred plan. Payment can be made online or at the dining office.
What if I have dietary restrictions?
We accommodate various dietary needs. Please submit a special request through the student dashboard or contact the dining manager directly at least 24 hours in advance to arrange special meals.
How can I check my meal balance?
Your current meal balance and transaction history are available in the "Billing" section of your student dashboard. You can also visit the dining office for a printed statement.
What is the procedure for guest meals?
Students can purchase guest meal tickets at the dining office. Guest meals cost ৳150 per meal and must be paid for in advance. Guests must be accompanied by the student.
