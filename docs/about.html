About Muktijuddha Hall Dining

Providing quality dining services to the students of CUET since 1995. Our mission is to nourish minds and build community through exceptional dining experiences.

Our Story

Muktijuddha Hall Dining was established in 1995 with a vision to provide nutritious, affordable, and culturally appropriate meals to the students of Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology (CUET). Named after the spirit of our Liberation War, we strive to uphold the values of resilience, community, and excellence in all our services.

Over the years, we have grown from a small dining facility serving 200 students to a comprehensive dining system catering to over 1,200 students daily. Our commitment to quality, hygiene, and student satisfaction has made us one of the most trusted dining services in the university.

1995

Inception

Muktijuddha Hall Dining established with basic meal services for 200 students.

2005

Expansion

Expanded services to accommodate 500 students with improved facilities.

2015

Digital Transformation

Implemented the first digital dining management system, serving 800 students.

2023

Modernization

Launched current comprehensive dining management system serving 1,200+ students.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide nutritious, affordable, and hygienic meals that support the academic and personal growth of every student while fostering a sense of community within the hall.

Our Vision

To become the benchmark for university dining services in Bangladesh, known for excellence in food quality, service delivery, and student satisfaction.

Our Values

Quality, Integrity, Innovation, Student-Centric Approach, and Community Building guide every aspect of our dining services.
